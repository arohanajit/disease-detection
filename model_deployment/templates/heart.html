Chest Pain Guide:
  • 0 -> typical angina
  • 1 -> atypical angina
  • 2 -> non-anginal pain
  • 3 -> asymptomatic






Resting Echocardiographic Measurement guide:
  • 0 -> Normal
  • 1 -> having ST-T wave abnormality
  • 2 -> showing probable or definite left ventricular hypertrophy by Estes' criteria






the slope of the peak exercise ST segment guide:
  • 0 -> Upsloping
  • 1 -> Flat
  • 2 -> Downsloping




Thalassemia guide:
  • 0 -> NA
  • 1 -> Normal
  • 2 -> Fixed Defect
  • 3 -> Reversable Defect
